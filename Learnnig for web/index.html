Nanjing University, commonly known as "NJU", is a prestigious century-old institution located in the culturally rich ancient capital of Nanjing. As a key university under China's "Double First-Class", "985 Project", and "211 Project" initiatives, it has always been committed to inheriting civilization, pursuing truth, cultivating talents, and serving society, enjoying a high reputation in higher education both domestically and internationally.

Its history dates back to Sanjiang Normal School founded in 1902, evolving through stages such as Liangjiang Normal School, Nanjing Higher Normal School, National Southeast University, and National Central University, before officially adopting the name Nanjing University in 1950. Throughout its over 100-year journey, the university has been closely linked to the fate of the nation, nurturing numerous outstanding alumni who have made remarkable contributions to national development and social progress.
